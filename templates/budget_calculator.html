Frugal Oat's Friendly Advice: A dollar saved is a dollar earned!


Of course money does not buy happiness... it buys tennis balls, which buys happiness!! :)

Now, although happiness and enjoying life is really important, you also need to remember to save! You're just one deflated tennis ball or punctured vape toy away from a disaster... trust me, I learned the hard way. You also never know when the insurance on your porsche might go up because your dog jumped between the pedals to chase a ball causing you to crash...

So, the most important thing you can do is to save a part of your child support checks for rainy days! You'll thank yourself so much for it!

*Just kidding, ignore all of this advice. There are literally no rainy days in Santa Barbara ever... go splurge on that lemon-lime vape flavor!
